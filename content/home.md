# Home Page

This file documents the intended content for the Edge8 homepage. It is not a strict schema. It is a plain-language reference so AI can understand the page and rebuild or revise it without needing frontmatter or section mapping.

## Page Intent

Edge8 helps organizations become Tech-Forward through AI Programs, Global Talent Staffing, and AI Officer Leadership. The homepage should feel clear, credible, modern, and conversion-focused.

Shared visual guidance: see `content/design-reference.md`.

Primary CTA: Schedule A Consultation

Primary CTA link: `#contact`

Hero media: `/images/edge8-hero.mp4`

## Audience

Founders, operators, and business leaders exploring structured AI adoption.

## Tone

Confident, practical, premium, and execution-focused.

Avoid hype-heavy or overly technical language.

## SEO

Primary keyword: AI Programs

Secondary keywords:

- AI Officer
- Tech-Forward
- AI consulting
- AI leadership

## Design Direction

Use the shared rules in `content/design-reference.md`.

For the homepage specifically:

- keep strong contrast between sections
- make CTAs obvious
- emphasize trust and proof
- let the page move from promise to proof to conversion
