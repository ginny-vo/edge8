## How Do AI Agents Improve Business Operations?

AI agents act as virtual team members:
- Handle repetitive tasks
- Manage workflows
- Deliver real-time insights
- Route information to right decision-makers
- Monitor and analyze data for trends
- Execute marketing tasks

This increases productivity while human teams focus on strategy.

---

## What Is an AI Program?

**Definition:** A structured set of AI-powered systems designed to improve business performance at scale. Combines AI agents for task automation, data analysis, and communication with tools supporting marketing, operations, and customer engagement.
