## How Does SEO and GEO Help Personal Brands Get Found?

- **SEO:** Ranks personal brand site in traditional search results
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization):** Ensures expertise appears in AI-driven platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)
- **Combined:** Content reaches people wherever they search for leadership insights

---

## What Is a Personal Brand Website?

**Definition:** Your professional home online, showcasing expertise, achievements, and leadership story. When combined with AI agents, it becomes a high-impact content engine producing SEO- and GEO-optimized content at scale while maintaining your authentic voice.

---

## Why Personal Branding With AI Agents?

AI agents streamline personal branding by:
- Drafting articles from short notes or transcripts
- Repurposing content across blog, social, newsletters
- Maintaining brand voice in every channel
- Suggesting topics aligned with your expertise

---

## Steps to Building a Personal Brand Website

1. Define strategic positioning and audience
2. Build story-driven homepage and about page
3. Publish GEO-optimized thought leadership with AI agent support
4. Create clear CTAs for speaking, consulting, partnerships
5. Maintain consistent publishing schedule powered by AI-assisted workflows

---

## Expected Results

- Increased visibility in search engines AND AI tools
- Stronger trust with clients and partners
- More inbound speaking, consulting, media opportunities
